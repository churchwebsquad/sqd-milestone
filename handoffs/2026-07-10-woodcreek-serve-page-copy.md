## Section 2 — How serving works (3 steps)

### 1. Raise your hand.

Pick where you want to serve — Hospitality, Worship Arts, Kids, Youth, Outreach — and submit that ministry's form. (For Worship Arts that's the audition form; for Kids/Youth it's the volunteer application.)

### 2. Get set up to serve.

Complete what that spot requires. For most roles that's becoming a member / attending Welcome to Church. For anything with kids or students, it also means MinistrySafe training and a background check. (Some Hospitality roles are open to anyone with a heart to serve — you don't have to be a member to start greeting or making coffee.)

### 3. Meet the team and jump in.

A short, real conversation with the ministry leader — an audition meeting with Jon, an interview with the Kidcreek/Youth director, a meet-and-greet — and then you start.
